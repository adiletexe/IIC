How to Analyze

To use Charts

1. Go to "Charts" page.

2. Click the search icon on the top-left corner in the chart widget.

3. Type one of: BTCUSDT, ETCUSDT, LTCUSDT, EOSUSDT, XRPUSDT, DASHUSDT and press "Enter".

4. Use the toolbar to choose Timeframe, Indicators, etc.

To Buy Assets

1. Go to "Profile" page.

2. Choose a crypto, refresh price, specify quantity, and click "Buy".
